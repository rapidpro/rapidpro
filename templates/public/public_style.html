Language
Baseline rules for how we talk about things with users.
Do not capitalize (except with title case) or hyphenate email.
Users "Sign In" and "Sign Out".
Account passwords are reset, not recovered.
For contacts, we have Groups and Smart Groups.
Capitalize Flows, Contacts, Campaigns, Groups, Smart Groups, Triggers
Users "Select" instead of "Choose", Select the Flow to start
Users "Connect" numbers to their account when adding a channel.
